What We Provide

TripMeNow is a tool with the purpose of helping you in your trip planning process with a simple click.
The app gives you your current location details which include; weather, attractions, restaurants, and hotels!
Also with a simple click you can load information from anywhere on the map!

Weather Forecast

Stay up to date with current Weather. Get the current day's hourly weather report. Switch between fahrenheit a celsius. Look at the 3 day forecast. As well as receive uv, wind, sunrise, sunset, and humidity information!

Attractions Restaurants & Hotels

Sort through local attractions restaurants and hotel as you plan your search. Don't forget to save items you like. All saved items will be stored in the saves section located below the map!

One Click Feature

Upon loading you will notice the local forecast, attractions, restaurants and hotels. However you are just one click away from discovering these items anywhere in the world. Simply pick a location a tap it! Make sure to zoom in for better accuracy!
